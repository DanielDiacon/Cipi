My Journey to Becoming a Graphic Designer.

Hello, I'm Ciprian, and I discovered my passion for graphic design after stumbling upon a few vintage posters on Pinterest. The stunning visuals and clever messaging immediately caught my attention, and I became fascinated by the artistry and creativity that went into their creation. From that moment on, I knew I wanted to pursue a career in graphic design.

To turn my dream into a reality, I enrolled in several courses to learn the fundamentals of Graphic Design. These courses provided me with a solid foundation in design principles, color theory, typography, and much more. As I delved deeper into the world of graphic design, I realized that it was not just a hobby, but a way of life. Every design challenge presented a new opportunity for me to flex my creative muscles and push myself to new heights.

It's a dream come true, and I feel grateful every day to be doing something that I love. I am constantly inspired by the endless possibilities of graphic design, and I am committed to continuing my growth and development as a designer for many years to come.

Skills
Photoshop
Photoshop is a photo editing software that allows you to manipulate and enhance images. It is used to adjust colors, remove unwanted elements, and create digital artwork.
Illustrator
Illustrator is a vector graphics editor used to create digital illustrations, logos, and other graphic designs. Unlike Photoshop, which deals with pixels, Illustrator works with mathematical equations to create scalable images.
InDesign
InDesign is a desktop publishing software used to create layouts for print and digital media. It is commonly used to create books, brochures, magazines, and other types of print media.
Affinity
Affinity is a suite of graphic design software that includes Photo (similar to Photoshop), Designer (similar to Illustrator), and Publisher (similar to InDesign). It is a more affordable alternative to the Adobe Creative Suite.
